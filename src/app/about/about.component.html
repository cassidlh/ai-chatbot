Lumina AI

Lumina AI is an adapting, emotionally intelligent chatbot created to deal with grief from losing a loved one. What sets us apart is that Lumina AI has ability to replicate conversations with a lost loved one, offering a comforting and familiar presence to aid in the grieving process. By utilizing past text exchanges between the user and their loved one, the chatbot generates responses that reflect the deceased person's communication style, fostering a deeply personal and supportive experience. While its interface is similar to existing AI chatbots, Lumina AI is uniquely designed to facilitate healing and emotional processing.

Beyond the chatbot feature, the Lumina website includes two additional sections. The "About" section provides an overview of Lumina AI, its purpose, and guidance on how to use it effectively. The "Resources" section offers external mental health support for those who may not yet feel ready to engage with the chatbot or who seek additional assistance beyond its capabilities.

Limitations

Lumina AI aims to provide accessible grief support, but several limitations must be considered. AI cannot fully replace human therapists, as complex grief often requires human intervention. Ethical concerns like data privacy, emotional dependence, and user expectations must be carefully managed. Overcoming linguistic and cultural barriers requires multilingual support and culturally aware responses. The chatbot's limited crisis intervention skills highlight the need for human support. Additionally, skepticism about AI for emotional support calls for trust-building through awareness and education.

Long Term Goals

Our long-term goals focus on enhancing Lumina AI's capabilities and reach. We aim to improve its language model and NLP for more empathetic conversations. Collaboration with mental health experts will refine responses for accuracy and effectiveness. Integrating Lumina AI with university mental health services will provide additional support alongside existing resources. Expanding language accessibility remains a priority. Finally, partnerships with universities and mental health organizations will drive outreach, credibility, and adoption, ensuring Lumina AI becomes a valuable grief support tool.
